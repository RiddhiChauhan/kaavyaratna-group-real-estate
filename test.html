The Fern Residency, Gandhinagar

The Fern Residency, Gandhinagar, situated at Sargasan Cross Road on Sarkhej-Gandhinagar Highway, offers 75 contemporary rooms and suites, all equipped with modern amenities. Our all-day dining restaurant caters to your culinary desires and we provide state-of-the-art banquets ideally suited for social or corporate events. Enjoy our well-equipped gym to rejuvenate during your stay. Centrally located at Sargasan Cross road, the hotel provides easy access to Mahatma Mandir Convention and Exhibition Centre, Info City IT Park, TCS Garima Park and other of Gandhinagar's most popular landmarks.
